Product Description

A Must-Have Waterproof Pouch to Protect Your Phone

UGREEN waterproof phone pouch is an excellent choice for 4" to 6.5" diagonal size smartphones, protecting cell phone against water, dust, dirt, snow or even scratches effectively.

With enough room inside, you can also put your credit card, hotel key, cash or even a slim wallet in the pouch, a reliable companion for fishing, swimming, boating, kayaking, snorkeling, and water park activities.

IPX8 Protection

UGREEN waterproof phone pouch is designed to survive tough conditions and withstand up to 100ft from water without any intrusion. Resist the entry of dust, snow, sand, dirt and especially water.

Easy Access to Fingerprints Touch ID

Innovative fingerprint area design allows you to operate all touch screen functions and unlock home button sensors easily without interruption. No need to take your phone out from the dry bag.

100% Sealed

Dual-side Transparent Window, Clear on both the front and back, allows easy access to take pictures or video, check emails or even answer a call.

Important Notes

  • Touchscreen functions and fingerprint sensor don't work underwater because of hydraulic pressure so please take photos by volume buttons in that case.
  • Please do a safety test with a cloth or tissue inside before directly putting your phone in this waterproof phone case.
  • Mobile phones with a thick protective case like Otterbox may not fit in this dry bag.
  • This waterproof case is suggested to use under fresh or saltwater, not for hot spring.
