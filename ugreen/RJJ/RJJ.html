USB C Card Reader to SD/TF Card Compatible with MacBook Pro, Macbook Air, Dell Xps 13/15, Galaxy Smartphone and Tablets

Dual Slots, Double Duty

The UGREEN SD/TF USB C card reader can simultaneously access data from two memory cards and exchange data directly between the two slots.

It supports popular memory cards like UHS-I SD, Micro SD, TF, SDXC, SDHC, Micro SDXC, Micro SDHC, RS-MMC, and MMC up to 2TB.

The extra USB female port allows you to connect USB peripherals such as USB flash disk, mouse, keyboard, card reader or wireless dongle for your keyboard/mouse.

Convenient and portable

  • You can use a USB-C card reader on an OTG enabled smartphone to share photos with your family and friends anytime, anywhere, so you can share the beautiful scenery with them as you travel.
  • The SD card reader is small and lightweight, making it ideal for carrying in a backpack or bag, the best choice for travel and business trips.

Friendly cable design

Compared to the wireless design, the flexible connection cable offers enough leeway to rotate the card reader in all directions and prevents neighboring USB ports on the laptop or computer from being blocked.

Fast Data Transfers to Increase Workflow Efficiency

This USB C card reader makes it easy to offload photos and video, providing fast access through USB C with file transfer speeds up to 5Gbps. Increase workflow efficiency for creative professionals such as photographers and videographers. It helps to increase your productivity by saving you time when offloading raw images or editing video and photos, and backing up files.

Kind Reminder

  • Please kindly know that Samsung smartphone only supports FAT32 and EXFAT, but don't support NTFS.
  • Please kindly make sure that your USB C phone/tablet supports OTG function before purchasing.
  • Only compatible with devices of Type C Thunderbolt 3 interface, NOT work with Lightning / Micro USB/USB A interface like iPhone, iPad.
