Product Description

UGREEN Adjustable Charging Stand for Nintendo Switch and lite

Are you always having trouble when taking the original dock on the go?

This charging stand replacement solves your problem perfectly, it also supports switch and switche lite console. Much smaller, enough to put in a pocket, easy to carry around with you when going out or traveling without having to carry the whole dock.

Notice:

1. This charging stand CANNOT support TV mode

2. To charge the switch or switch lite, an extra power source is needed. (There's no AC Adapter in the package) This switch stand can support original AC adapter and our AC adapter.

3. You need to remove the case to use this switch charging stand.

Read more

Plug and Play

This switch charging dock releases your hands while playing games and holds Switch upright without worrying about the low battery. The kickstand lets you play favorite switch games while charging, make the game unstoppable.

Anti Sliding Pad

Prevent your switch from shaking and slipping, protect your switch from the details.

The angle of the stand could be adjusted to 0°~100° to create the best viewing angle for different environments. Allows you to play your favorite game in tabletop mode at any angle while it's on charging through AC adapter.
