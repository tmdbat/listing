Product Description

UGREEN Carrying Case Plus Protective Case Cover and Tempered Glass Screen Protector for Nintendo Switch Lite 2019

Excellent Protection

Offers great protection to your Nintendo Switch Lite. The outer hard shell protects the machine from drops. Inner soft microfiber lining gives your Switch Lite a snug and comfortable home. A central soft flap protects the screen from being scratched inside the case.

Hard and Durable

Designed to make your new Nintendo Switch Lite even more portable & travel-friendly. The durable hardshell case protects the Nintendo Switch from drops and scratches. Two Straps allow your Nintendo Switch lite to sit flat in the case. There is no zipper on the inside mesh netting, so no need to worry about the Switch getting scratched while inside.

Ultra Slim

It is very slim and super light. An adjustable and removable hand strap allows comfortable holding or carrying. You can also put it in your backpack to take anywhere

Organized Storage Case

A large mesh pocket provides a secure storage room for some small Nintendo accessories like charging cable, earbuds and some accessories. Easier to Close and Easier to Carry. It also builds with 5 game card slots, allowing you to carry and change your favorite games anytime & anywhere.
